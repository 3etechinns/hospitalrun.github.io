HospitalRun is an offline-first Ember application.

For the Developing World

HospitalRun is focused on providing the best health platform possible to the least resourced environments.

Offline Enabled

HospitalRun was designed to allow records to be carried to remote clinics. It works when there is no Internet, and syncs when there is.

Intuitive

HospitalRun is focused on being intuitive for clincians, administrators, and even contributors.

Saving Time

HospitalRun focuses on optimizing the one resource we can't make more of: people's time.

More than a Download

We're committed to making HospitalRun - not just a piece of software - but an open source service focused on the needs of developing world health care facilities.

A Platform for Partnering

HospitalRun seeks to bring people and organizations from the NGO, government, and technology sectors together to solve a problem that just might change the world.

"It's about seeing the constraints of lower-resource settings as a driver - rather than a constraint - for innovation."
