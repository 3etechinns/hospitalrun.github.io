Free & Open Source

HospitalRun is free to install and use, and has no licensing costs. It is actively developed as an open source project on GitHub.

Offline Enabled

HospitalRun was designed to allow records to be carried to remote clinics. It works when there is no internet, and syncs when there is.

Inventory & Billing

HospitalRun allows you to manage the business of running your hospital with patient records, inventory, billing, and more.

Intuitive Interface

HospitalRun is architected for resource constrained environments and variable connectivity with a focus on being intuitive.
